The process

Six controlled steps, one custom result.

  1. 1

    Order the impression kit

    Receive putty, return supplies, and instructions for the selected toenails or fingernails.

  2. 2

    Prepare the putty

    Follow the included timing and mixing instructions before placing the material over the nail.

  3. 3

    Capture each nail

    Place the putty over the nail, apply pressure, and allow the impression to form.

  4. 4

    Number the impressions

    Mark the impressions in order so each finished tray matches the correct nail.

  5. 5

    Send photos for approval

    Photos are reviewed before the impressions are mailed back, helping reduce remakes and delays.

  6. 6

    Receive finished trays

    Approved impressions are used to create trays shaped for your exact nail anatomy.
