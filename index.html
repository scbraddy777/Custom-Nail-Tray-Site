The difference

Treatment products work best when they stay where you put them.

Custom Nail Tray is shaped around the customer, not a generic mold. The tray creates a more controlled way to hold nail care products against the nail surface while reducing shifting, smearing, and wasted product.

  • Built-in reservoir helps hold product at the nail plate.
  • Custom fit helps make longer wear easier to keep up with.
  • At-home photo review helps confirm impressions before mailing.
