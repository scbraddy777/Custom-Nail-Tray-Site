FAQ

Questions customers are likely to ask first.

Is this a medication?

No. Custom Nail Tray is a custom-fit tray system designed to hold nail care products against the nail. Treatments and medications are separate.

Do I need to visit a clinic?

No. The brochure describes an at-home impression process with photos reviewed before you send impressions back.

Can I use it for fingernails too?

Yes. The brochure references both toe and finger nails, depending on the care routine and impression set submitted.

What if I mess up the impression?

The brochure says replacement supplies can be provided after you send impression photos for review.
