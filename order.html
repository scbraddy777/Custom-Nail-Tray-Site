Questions

Clear answers before customers order.

Is Custom Nail Tray a medication?

No. Custom Nail Tray is a custom-fit tray system designed to be used with the nail care product a customer chooses.

Can it be used with antifungal ointment?

Yes, it is designed to help hold topical products closer to the nail plate as part of a consistent care routine.

What if the impression does not come out right?

Customers can send photos for review before mailing impressions back, and replacement supplies are available when a first attempt needs to be redone.

Does it work for toenails and fingernails?

Yes. The kit can be used for toe or fingernail impressions so the finished tray can match the selected nail shape.

Can I use any nail care product with it?

The tray is intended to support topical nail care products, but customers should follow the directions for the product they choose.
